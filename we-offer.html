Services I Offer

At Attaboy Dog Training, I know that every dog is different. Whether you’ve got a lively pup learning the basics, a teenager pushing boundaries, or an older dog needing a fresh approach to tackle bad habits, I’m here to help. Together, we’ll come up with the perfect training plan for you and your dog.

Puppy Training

I have the perfect four-week basic obedience course for puppies under six months. This will take place in your home and surrounding area once a week for four weeks. Each session will be 60-90mins.

The pup will learn:

  • Focus & Attention
  • Go to “Bed”
  • Sit – Down – Stay
  • Recall
  • Leave it
  • Leash manners

We will teach calm around food and stomp out bad behaviours like nipping, jumping and whining. We will discuss best practices on socialisation, crate training and tackle any potty training issues.

Basic Obedience Training

Suitable for dogs of all ages, this programme covers essential commands and lays the groundwork for a well-behaved family dog.

Features Include:

  • Teaching proper leash manners and loose-lead walking.
  • Reliable recall techniques for better off-lead control.
  • Sit, stay, down, and leave-it commands.
  • Tackling nuisance behaviours like jumping, barking, and chewing.

Tailored to fit your lifestyle, we work in your home and around the area at a time that suits you.

Behaviour Modification

I help dogs overcome reactivity, anxiety, or problem behaviours with one-to-one personalised plans.

Features Include:

  • In-depth assessments to get to the root of the problem.
  • Training strategies to reduce reactivity and build confidence.
  • Desensitisation techniques for triggers like noises or other dogs.
  • Step-by-step support to create lasting behavioural change.
  • Ongoing guidance to track progress and adjust as needed.
